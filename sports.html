I have many hobbies/interests and have taken part in many sports over the years such as hockey, tennis, basketball etc. but my main passion would be horse riding;
which I have done from a very young age. I regularly compete in Showjumping competitions and also breed pedigree horses in my family's equestrian centre.

There are many professional showjumpers I admire such as Cian O'Connor, Bertram Allen, Jessica Curtin and also a good friend of mine, Shane O'Reilly.
I feel that sometimes the horse riding industry isn't given enough credit as a sport despite the immense dedication and discipline it envolves.
Contrary to popular belief, horse riding in general is a tough sport and requires a lot of bravery as you are putting your complete trust in a huge creature to translate your commands and execute them safely and with accuracy.
