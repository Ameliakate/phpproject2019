Immersion


Immersion (virtual reality) has long been a popluar technology. Technology has been able to master the art of
perception, immersion wise. This has been done by making a person feel like they are physically present in a non-physical world.
This has been done by surrounding the user of the Visual Reality system in images, sound or other stimuli that provide an engrossing,
almost addictive alternate reality.
